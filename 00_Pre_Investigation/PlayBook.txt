8. Manual Triage: High-Value Artifact Review
The text-based outputs from scripts 4 and 5 are designed for rapid human inspection. During review, prioritize identifying the following Indicators of Compromise (IOCs):

Privileged_Group_Audit.txt: Scan for Anomalous or "Generic" Accounts (e.g., Admin1, Support, IT_Service) that do not align with the organization’s baseline or naming conventions.

Scheduled_Tasks_Audit: Identify Persistence Mechanisms utilizing encoded commands, specifically searching for strings containing powershell.exe -enc.

Remote_Process_Scan: Flag Suspicious Execution Paths: Monitor for processes running from volatile directories like \Temp\, \Public\, or \AppData\.

Look for Masquerading Processes: Identify binaries missing a valid 'Company' Description or a verified digital signature.
